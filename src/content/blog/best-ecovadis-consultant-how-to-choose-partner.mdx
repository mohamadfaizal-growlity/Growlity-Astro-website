FAQs

### What does the best EcoVadis consultant actually do?

The best EcoVadis consultant supports organizations across the full assessment lifecycle, rather than focusing only on submission. This typically begins with a structured gap analysis to evaluate current ESG maturity against EcoVadis scoring criteria. From there, consultants assist in developing policies, aligning documentation with key themes such as Environment, Labor & Human Rights, Ethics, and Sustainable Procurement, and organizing evidence for submission. In more advanced engagements, consultants also help establish governance systems, supplier sustainability processes, and continuous improvement frameworks. This ensures that companies are not only prepared for a single assessment but are positioned for consistent long-term performance.

### How to choose the best EcoVadis consultant for your company ?

Selecting the best EcoVadis consultant depends on multiple factors, including your organization’s size, industry, and internal ESG capabilities. Companies typically evaluate consultants based on their methodology, experience with EcoVadis assessments, and ability to provide hands-on documentation support. A strong consultant should offer a structured approach, demonstrate familiarity with global ESG frameworks, and provide post-assessment improvement strategies. Organizations also benefit from working with consultants who understand sector-specific risks and compliance expectations.

### Is hiring an EcoVadis consultant necessary?

Hiring an EcoVadis consultant is not mandatory, but it can significantly improve the efficiency and effectiveness of the assessment process. Organizations with limited internal ESG expertise or those undergoing their first EcoVadis assessment often benefit the most from external support. Consultants help streamline documentation, reduce common errors, and ensure alignment with scoring expectations. Even companies with internal sustainability teams may engage consultants to validate their approach and strengthen their submission.

### Can an EcoVadis consultant guarantee a medal outcome?

No responsible EcoVadis consultant guarantees a specific medal outcome such as Gold or Platinum. The final score depends on several factors, including the organization’s existing ESG systems, quality of implementation, and strength of supporting documentation. Consultants play a critical role in improving readiness and aligning submissions with evaluation criteria, but outcomes ultimately depend on the organization’s operational maturity and evidence quality.

### How long does it take to prepare for an EcoVadis assessment?

The preparation timeline for an EcoVadis assessment typically ranges from 8 to 16 weeks. This depends on factors such as company size, availability of data, and the maturity of existing ESG policies and processes. Organizations with well-documented systems may complete the process faster, while those building policies from scratch may require additional time for development and implementation.

### What is the difference between an EcoVadis consultant and a general ESG consultant?

An EcoVadis consultant focuses specifically on helping organizations prepare for and improve their EcoVadis assessment. This includes aligning documentation with scoring themes and optimizing submission quality. In contrast, a general ESG consultant provides broader advisory services, such as sustainability reporting, climate strategy, and governance system design. In many cases, organizations work with firms that combine both capabilities.

### How much does an EcoVadis consultant cost?

The cost of engaging an EcoVadis consultant varies depending on the scope of work, company size, and level of support required. Engagements may range from targeted gap assessments to full-cycle advisory covering documentation, submission, and improvement strategy. Organizations typically evaluate cost based on the value of improved ratings, enhanced compliance, and increased access to global supply chains.

### Can small and mid-sized companies benefit from EcoVadis consulting?

Small and mid-sized enterprises often benefit significantly from EcoVadis consulting services, especially if they do not have dedicated ESG teams. Consultants help structure the process, prioritize actions, and improve documentation quality. This enables smaller organizations to compete more effectively in global markets where sustainability ratings play an important role.

### What should companies evaluate before hiring an EcoVadis consultant?

Before selecting a consultant, organizations typically assess factors such as methodology, industry experience, documentation support capabilities, and post-assessment services. It is also useful to understand whether the consultant can align with international frameworks and provide examples of previous engagements. A well-aligned consultant should be able to support both immediate assessment goals and long-term sustainability development.
