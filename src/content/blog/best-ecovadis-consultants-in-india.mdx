## **3 Critical Pitfalls to Avoid in an EcoVadis Submission**

1.  **Treating it as an HR or EHS Silo:** EcoVadis requires cross-functional data. If your HR team doesn’t communicate with procurement or operations, your evidence will be disjointed and inconsistent.
2.  **Uploading Expired or Undated Proof:** Documents must show active usage within recent cycles. Uploading a training manual from five years ago without recent attendance sheets will lead to zero scoring credit for that module.
3.  **Ignoring the CAP (Corrective Action Plan):** If you are re-submitting to improve a previous score, you must show clear progress on the platform’s custom-generated Corrective Action Plan. Ignoring past feedback signals a lack of management maturity to the auditors.
