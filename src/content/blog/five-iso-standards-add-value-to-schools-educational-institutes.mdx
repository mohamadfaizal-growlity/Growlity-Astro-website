The best resource to know this list of standards is the website of [iso.org](https://www.iso.org/home.html) itself.

You will find thousands of standards over this website but few important for schools and educational institutes are listed as below:

## [ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management System](https://www.iso.org/standard/62085.html)

![iso 9001](https://www.growlity.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/05/ISO-9001.jpg "ISO 9001")

ISO 9001:2015 specifies requirements for a quality management system when an organization:

*   needs to demonstrate its ability to consistently provide products and services that meet customer and applicable statutory and regulatory requirements, and
*   aims to enhance customer satisfaction through the effective application of the system, including processes for improvement of the system and the assurance of conformity to customer and applicable statutory and regulatory requirements.

## [ISO 14001:2015 Quality Management System](https://www.iso.org/standard/60857.html)

![iso 14001](https://www.growlity.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/05/ISO-14001.jpg "ISO 14001")

ISO 14001:2015 helps an organization achieve the intended outcomes of its environmental management system, which provide value for the environment, the organization itself and interested parties. [](https://www.iso.org/standard/63787.html)

Consistent with the organization’s environmental policy, the intended outcomes of an environmental management system include:

*   enhancement of environmental performance;
*   fulfilment of compliance obligations;
*   achievement of environmental objectives.

## [ISO 45001:2015 Occupational Health & Safety Management System](https://www.iso.org/standard/63787.html)

![iso 45001](https://www.growlity.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/05/ISO-45001.jpg "ISO 45001")

ISO 45001:2018 helps an organization to achieve the intended outcomes of its OH&S management system.

Consistent with the organization’s OH&S policy, the intended outcomes of an OH&S management system include:

*   continual improvement of OH&S performance;
*   fulfilment of legal requirements and other requirements;
*   achievement of OH&S objectives.

## [ISO 50001:2018 Energy Management System](https://www.iso.org/iso-50001-energy-management.html)

![iso 50001](https://www.growlity.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/05/ISO-50001.jpg "ISO 50001")

The intended outcome of ISO 5001:2018 is to enable an organization to follow a systematic approach in achieving continual improvement of energy performance. 

This Standard:

*   is applicable to any organization regardless of its type, size, complexity, geographical location, organizational culture or the products and services it provides;
*   is applicable to activities affecting energy performance that are managed and controlled by the organization;
*   is applicable irrespective of the quantity, use, or types of energy consumed; 

## [ISO 21001:2018 Management System for Educational Organizations](https://www.iso.org/standard/66266.html)

![iso 21001](https://www.growlity.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/05/ISO-21001.jpg "ISO 21001")

ISO 21001:2018 specifies requirements for a management system for educational organizations (EOMS) when such an organization:

*   needs to demonstrate its ability to support the acquisition and development of competence through teaching, learning or research;
*   aims to enhance satisfaction of learners, other beneficiaries and staff through the effective application of its EOMS, including processes for improvement of the system and assurance of conformity to the requirements of learners and other beneficiaries.
