## **3 Common Reasons EcoVadis Scores Stall**

### **1\. Policies Exist but Lack Supporting Evidence**

Many organizations have sustainability, ethics, or environmental policies in place. However, it requires evidence that these policies are actively implemented.

Examples include:

*   Employee training records
*   Internal audits
*   Performance KPIs
*   Supplier assessments
*   Management reviews
*   Improvement initiatives

Without supporting evidence, even strong policies may have limited impact on overall scoring.

Proper evidence mapping is often the difference between an average score and a leading score.

FAQs

### What does an EcoVadis consultant do?

An EcoVadis consultant helps organizations prepare for EcoVadis assessments by evaluating existing sustainability practices, identifying gaps, developing required policies and documentation, mapping evidence, improving ESG processes, and supporting assessment submissions. The objective is to improve sustainability performance and achieve a stronger EcoVadis rating.

### How long does it take to improve an EcoVadis rating?

The timeline depends on the organization’s current sustainability maturity. Companies with established ESG programs may see improvements within one assessment cycle, while organizations building sustainability systems from scratch may require several months to implement policies, collect evidence, and demonstrate performance improvements.

### Is EcoVadis mandatory for suppliers?

EcoVadis is not legally mandatory. However, many multinational companies and procurement teams require suppliers to complete assessments as part of their supplier evaluation and sustainability due diligence processes. A strong EcoVadis rating can improve competitiveness and increase opportunities with global customers.

### What documents are required for an EcoVadis assessment?

Typical documents include sustainability policies, environmental policies, codes of conduct, ethics policies, labor and human rights policies, supplier management procedures, training records, certifications, audit reports, environmental performance data, and ESG-related evidence. The exact requirements depend on company size, industry, and assessment scope.

### How much does EcoVadis consulting cost in the USA?

Consulting costs vary depending on company size, industry complexity, assessment maturity, and required support. Some organizations only need assessment guidance, while others require end-to-end sustainability strategy, documentation development, and implementation support. The best approach is to request a customized assessment of your requirements.

### What industries benefit most from EcoVadis consulting?

Consulting is valuable for manufacturing, pharmaceuticals, chemicals, automotive, logistics, consumer goods, technology, packaging, construction, food and beverage, and other sectors that operate within global supply chains. Any organization facing customer sustainability requirements can benefit from support.

### Why is EcoVadis important for winning new business?

Many procurement teams use ratings as a risk management and supplier evaluation tool.
