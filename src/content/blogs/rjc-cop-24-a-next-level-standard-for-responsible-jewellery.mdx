In November 2024, the Responsible Jewellery Council (RJC) approved the Code of Practices 2024 (COP 24)—the most comprehensive update since COP 19 in 2019. This refresh reflects stakeholder feedback, emerging risks, and global sustainability shifts.

  
COP 24 now includes 45 provisions, structured to cover six core objectives: legal compliance; health & safety; human rights & supply chain diligence; product integrity; labour & working conditions; and responsible mining & environment.

## **COP 19 vs COP 24: What’s New?**

![rjc-cop-19-vs-cop-24](https://www.growlity.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/rjc-cop-19-vs-cop-24.png "rjc-cop-19-vs-cop-24")

These enhancements mean COP 24 isn’t just a policy update—it’s a strategic framework that  
mandates evidence-based implementation and transparency.

## **Why COP 24 Unlocks Value for Jewellery Businesses**

*   **Reduces business, reputational & regulatory risks**
*   **Builds consumer trust**—modern buyers insist on ethical, traceable sourcing
*   **Aligns with investor expectations**—ESG scoring, SDG-linked standards
*   **Prepares you for future regulation** (e.g., EU Corporate Sustainability Due Diligence)
*   **Validates leadership in ESG and innovation**—especially with lab-grown and recycled materials
