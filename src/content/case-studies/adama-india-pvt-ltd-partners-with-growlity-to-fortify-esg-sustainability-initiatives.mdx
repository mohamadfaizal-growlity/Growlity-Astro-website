Dahej, Gujarat, India, 04 January 2024

[Adama India Pvt. Ltd.](https://www.adama.com/india/en), the esteemed Indian subsidiary of ADAMA, a global leader in developing and distributing innovative agricultural solutions such as Fungicides, Insecticides, Herbicides, and Crop Enhancement products, proudly announces its collaboration with [Growlity](https://www.growlity.com/). This strategic partnership marks a significant step towards reinforcing Adama India’s commitment to [Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG)](https://www.growlity.com/consulting/) principles and sustainable business practices.

As part of its global commitment to responsible business conduct, [ADAMA](https://www.adama.com/india/en) seeks to embed sustainability at the heart of its operations. The alliance with [Growlity](https://www.growlity.com/) as their ESG & Sustainability Partner underscores Adama India’s dedication to fostering a sustainable and responsible corporate culture.

![](https://www.growlity.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/Adama-01.jpeg "Adama 01")

![](https://www.growlity.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/Adama-02.jpeg "Adama 02")

[Mr. Hitul Joshi](https://www.linkedin.com/in/hitul-joshi-csp-18582515/), HSE Manager & [Mrs. Vishwa Pandya](https://www.linkedin.com/in/vishwa-pandya-n%C3%A9e-joshi-209095129/), OHS Engineer at ADAMA India, expressed enthusiasm about the collaboration, stating, “At [ADAMA](https://www.adama.com/india/en), sustainability is a core value driving our operations. Partnering with [Growlity](https://www.growlity.com/) allows us to further enhance our commitment to ESG principles and catalyze our sustainability efforts in the Indian market.”

The collaboration between [Adama India Pvt. Ltd.](https://www.adama.com/india/en) and [Growlity](https://www.growlity.com/) will encompass various facets of sustainability. A key focus will be placed on accurately calculating greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions. Growlity’s team of seasoned domain experts will work closely with Adama India to derive precise [GHG emission calculations](https://www.growlity.com/carbon-neutrality/), setting the foundation for targeted emission reduction strategies.

![](https://www.growlity.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/Adama-03.jpeg "Adama 03")

![](https://www.growlity.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/Adama-04.jpeg "Adama 04")

Additionally, [Growlity](https://www.growlity.com/) will conduct comprehensive training sessions covering diverse topics of ESG and sustainability. These specialized training modules aim to equip Adama India’s workforce with the knowledge and tools necessary to integrate sustainable practices effectively into their day-to-day operations.

Commenting on the partnership, the CEO of Growlity, [Dr. Nitin Dumasia](https://www.linkedin.com/in/dr-nitin-dumasia-b6182616/), stated, “We are honored to collaborate with [Adama India Pvt. Ltd.](https://www.linkedin.com/in/dr-nitin-dumasia-b6182616/) in their sustainability journey. Our expertise in [ESG](https://www.growlity.com/consulting/) solutions combined with ADAMA’s commitment to innovation and sustainability will undoubtedly create a positive impact on the Indian agricultural sector.”

![](https://www.growlity.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/Adama-05.jpeg "Adama 05")

![](https://www.growlity.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/Adama-06.jpeg "Adama 06")

The partnership signifies a concerted effort to align [Adama India Pvt. Ltd.’s](https://www.adama.com/india/en) operations with global best practices in sustainability. By integrating ESG principles into its business strategy, Adama India aims to contribute to a more sustainable and resilient agricultural ecosystem.
