[APackaging Group LLC](https://apackaginggroup.com/), a global leader in innovative and sustainable packaging solutions, has been awarded the prestigious EcoVadis Platinum Medal**,** placing it in the top 1% of more than 130,000 companies assessed worldwide by EcoVadis — the leading global standard for business sustainability ratings.

This Group-level Platinum recognition validates APackaging Group’s unwavering commitment to Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) excellence across its operations. The Group achieved a balanced and outstanding performance in all four EcoVadis assessment pillars:

**Environment:** 80/100

**Labour & Human Rights:** 80/100

**Ethics:** 80/100

**Sustainable Procurement:** 80/100

With an overall score of 80/100 and placement in the 99th percentile globally, this milestone represents a major validation of APackaging Group’s responsible business practices and global ESG alignment. The success was made possible through a structured sustainability roadmap developed in collaboration with Growlity, Inc., a globally trusted ESG and sustainability consulting firm.
