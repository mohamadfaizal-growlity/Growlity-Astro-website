## **Setting New Benchmarks in Sustainable Packaging Excellence**

APackaging Group LLC, a global leader in custom packaging solutions and supply chain packaging services, continues to maintain the prestigious **EcoVadis Platinum Medal for the second consecutive assessment cycle**, positioning the company among **the top 1%** of more than **150,000 companies** evaluated worldwide across all industries.

With an outstanding overall EcoVadis score of **95/100** and a **99th percentile ranking**, APackaging Group reinforces its long-term commitment to environmental stewardship, ethical business conduct, responsible sourcing, and social responsibility across its global operations. The achievement reflects not only a milestone, but the company’s ongoing effort to consistently sustain the highest level of ESG performance year after year.

The recognition highlights APackaging Group’s fully integrated sustainability strategy — embedding responsible practices into every stage of the packaging lifecycle, from eco-design and material selection to manufacturing oversight, supplier management, and global distribution. The company continues to work with partners and brands to reduce environmental impact while maintaining product performance, quality, and regulatory compliance.

By sustaining this group-level Platinum rating, APackaging Group strengthens its mission to deliver packaging solutions that not only protect products but also actively contribute to a more circular and responsible global economy. The company remains committed to continuously improving its sustainability performance to preserve and advance this recognition in the years ahead.
