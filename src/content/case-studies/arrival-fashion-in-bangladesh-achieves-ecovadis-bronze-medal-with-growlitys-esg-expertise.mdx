[Arrival Fashion](https://www.arrivalfashion.com/), a leading garments and textile manufacturer in Dhaka, has earned the EcoVadis Bronze Medal 2025, a prestigious recognition for its sustainability performance. This milestone was achieved through [ESG advisory](https://www.growlity.com/consulting/) and strategic support from Growlity, Inc. USA, a global leader in [EcoVadis consulting and sustainability advisory services.](https://ecovadis.com/training-partners/)

The EcoVadis Bronze Medal places Arrival Fashion in the top 35% of companies assessed globally by EcoVadis 2025, the world’s most trusted provider of business sustainability ratings. The assessment covered critical ESG criteria including Environment, Labor & Human Rights, Ethics, and Sustainable Procurement.

## **Strengthening Sustainable Supply Chains**

Growlity was running a large-scale Sustainable Procurement Program for a European retail major, aimed at enhancing ESG performance across its vendor partners in India and Bangladesh. As one of the top garment manufacturers and exporters from Bangladesh to this retailer, Arrival Fashion was selected to undergo an EcoVadis assessment as part of the program.

Growlity began its partnership with Arrival Fashion in September 2024, offering full-spectrum EcoVadis support services, from ESG gap assessment, document preparation, and policy alignment to performance tracking. The assessment was successfully completed in a short period, showcasing Growlity’s efficiency and commitment to driving sustainability in global supply chains.
