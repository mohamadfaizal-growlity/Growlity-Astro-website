[Growlity](https://www.growlity.com/), a global leader in [ESG](https://www.growlity.com/consulting/) & [Sustainability Management advisory](https://www.growlity.com/strategy/), proudly announces the successful re-certification of Dholakia Technologies LLP and their globally recognized brand, [Craft Lab Grown Diamonds](https://www.craftdiamonds.co/), under the prestigious [SCS-007 Sustainability Rated Diamonds Certification](https://www.scsglobalservices.com/services/certified-sustainability-rated-diamonds) by [SCS Global Services](https://www.scsglobalservices.com/), California, USA.

This achievement underscores Craft Lab Grown Diamonds’ unwavering commitment to environmental stewardship, ethical responsibility, and climate-conscious luxury. The SCS-007 Certification is the most rigorous sustainability standard for the diamond industry, ensuring transparency, [climate neutrality](https://www.growlity.com/carbon-neutrality/), and social responsibility throughout the value chain.

### **Setting a Benchmark in Sustainability**

The SCS-007 Certification process is a meticulous and comprehensive evaluation, covering key pillars such as verified origin traceability, climate neutrality, ethical stewardship, sustainable production practices, and comprehensive sustainability investments. This standard sets an exceptional benchmark for the lab-grown diamond industry, reassuring consumers that each diamond they purchase is not just beautiful but also responsibly sourced.
