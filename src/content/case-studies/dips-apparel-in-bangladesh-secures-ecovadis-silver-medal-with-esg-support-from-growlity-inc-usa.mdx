In a significant achievement for sustainability in the apparel industry, [Dips Apparel Ltd](https://www.dipsbd.com/)., one of Bangladesh’s leading garment manufacturers, has earned the EcoVadis Silver Medal in the global EcoVadis Sustainability Rating. The accomplishment reflects the company’s commitment to ethical, environmental, and responsible business practices.

### **A Strategic Partnership Driving Sustainable Transformation**

This recognition was part of a broader Sustainable Procurement Program initiated by [Growlity](https://www.growlity.com/ecovadis-assessment/), Inc. USA, a global ESG and sustainability advisory firm. Growlity was executing a large-scale program for a European retail major, providing EcoVadis assessment support to key vendor partners across India and Bangladesh. As a top supplier of garments from Bangladesh to the retailer, Dips Apparel was selected to participate in the assessment process.

Growlity began the engagement with Dips Apparel in July 2024 and completed the EcoVadis submission support within 10–12 weeks, by November 2024. The collaboration included comprehensive ESG strategy development, evidence collection including Green House Gas Emission data, gap analysis, and alignment with EcoVadis sustainability criteria, covering Environment, Labor & Human Rights, Ethics, and Sustainable Procurement.

### **Silver Medal Recognition Boosts Global Visibility**

Achieving the Silver Medal places Dips Apparel among the top 15% of companies evaluated by [EcoVadis globally](https://ecovadis.com/). It also enhances their visibility with international buyers focused on sustainable sourcing and strengthens their position in the global textile and apparel supply chain.  
Growlity’s expertise in sustainability ratings, EcoVadis consulting, and ESG compliance frameworks continues to empower businesses across sectors to meet evolving global expectations and drive value through responsible practices.

### **About Dips Apparel Ltd.**

Dips Apparel is a reputed garment manufacturer in Bangladesh, providing high-quality apparel to international markets. The company is known for its focus on ethical production, environmental sustainability, and strong labor standards.

### **About Growlity, Inc. USA**

Growlity, Inc. USA is a leading global ESG and Sustainability advisory firm, supporting organizations in over 25 countries. With deep expertise in EcoVadis ratings, ESG reporting, and sustainable procurement programs, Growlity helps clients elevate their sustainability performance and meet global benchmarks.
