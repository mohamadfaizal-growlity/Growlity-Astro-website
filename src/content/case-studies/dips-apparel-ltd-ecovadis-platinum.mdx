[Dips Apparel Ltd.](https://www.dipsbd.com/), manufacturer in the ready-made garment (RMG) sector, has been awarded the **EcoVadis Platinum Medal**, the highest level of recognition for sustainability performance. This achievement places Dips Apparel among the **top 1% of companies globally** assessed by EcoVadis, out of more than **130,000 organizations evaluated across 180+ countries and 220+ industry sectors**, reflecting strong and consistent performance across environmental, social, ethical, and supply-chain sustainability criteria.

The Platinum recognition reflects [](https://www.dipsbd.com/)[Dips Apparel Ltd.’s](https://www.dipsbd.com/) structured approach to sustainability, supported by robust governance systems, transparent reporting practices, and continuous improvement initiatives across its operations and supply chain. The company demonstrated strong alignment with international ESG expectations, addressing key focus areas such as environmental management, labor and human rights, business ethics, and responsible procurement.

EcoVadis Performance Highlights

*   🌿 **Environment:** 87 / 100
*   👥 **Labor & Human Rights:** 86 / 100 
*   ⚖️ **Ethics:** 82 / 100 
*   🔗 **Sustainable Procurement:** 79 / 100 

📊 **Overall Score:** 85 / 100  
🏅 **Global Percentile Ranking:** 99+ 

These outstanding results underscore [Dips Apparel Ltd.’s](https://www.dipsbd.com/) position as a sustainability leader within the global apparel   
manufacturing industry. Achieving EcoVadis Platinum status requires not only strong policies and compliance   
mechanisms but also a deeply embedded culture of environmental responsibility, ethical business conduct and  continuous improvement across the organization.  

[Dips Apparel Ltd.’s](https://www.dipsbd.com/) success was achieved with the strategic support of **Growlity**, a global ESG and sustainability consulting  firm. Growlity played a pivotal role throughout the EcoVadis assessment journey—supporting framework alignment,strengthening documentation, validating data, enhancing performance metrics, and enabling transparent and  audit-ready sustainability reporting.
