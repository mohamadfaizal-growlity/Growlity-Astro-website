New York, USA – October 25, 2023

[Fusion Park LLC](https://fusionpark.us/), founded to help climate tech companies with impactful, commercially ready technologies accelerate its growth and impact in the most timely and sustainable manner, is pleased to announce the appointment of [Dr. Nitin Dumasia](https://www.linkedin.com/in/dr-nitin-dumasia-b6182616/) as its Global Sustainability Advisor.

[Dr. Nitin Dumasia](https://www.linkedin.com/in/dr-nitin-dumasia-b6182616/) is a renowned ESG & Business Sustainability expert with over two decades of experience in the field. He holds a Ph.D. in ESG & Business Sustainability Management and has worked in various capacities to address pressing sustainability challenges. [Dr. Dumasia](https://www.linkedin.com/in/dr-nitin-dumasia-b6182616/) is President & CEO of [Growlity, Inc.](https://www.growlity.com/) which is a USA based advisory company for [Environmental, Social & Governance (ESG)](https://www.growlity.com/consulting/) related services across the world. [Dr. Dumasia](https://www.linkedin.com/in/dr-nitin-dumasia-b6182616/) brings a wealth of knowledge and experience to this role and will play a pivotal part in advancing [Fusion Park](https://fusionpark.us/)‘s commitment to global sustainability and environmental stewardship.

In his new role at [Fusion Park](https://fusionpark.us/), [Dr. Dumasia](https://www.linkedin.com/in/dr-nitin-dumasia-b6182616/) will be responsible for shaping and driving the client’s sustainability strategy. This will include the development and execution of initiatives aimed at reducing the company’s carbon footprint, enhancing its commitment to renewable energy, and fostering a culture of environmental responsibility.

[Fusion Park](https://fusionpark.us/)‘s Founder & Managing Partner, [Mr. James Tu](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jamestunyc/), stated, “We are delighted to welcome [Dr. Nitin Dumasia](https://www.linkedin.com/in/dr-nitin-dumasia-b6182616/) to the [Fusion Park](https://fusionpark.us/) family. His vast experience and expertise in ESG & Sustainability make him the ideal addition to guide our global sustainability efforts. [Fusion Park LLC](https://fusionpark.us/) aims to be the trusted partner for companies that have brilliant people, big ideas, commercially ready technologies and burning passion on making positive changes to the world’s environment and sustainability. In other words, we want to accelerate positive change the climate tech companies bring for humanity to mitigate and adapt to climate change”.

[Dr. Dumasia](https://www.linkedin.com/in/dr-nitin-dumasia-b6182616/) expressed his enthusiasm about the this responsibility, saying, “I’m honored to onboard Fusion Park, a company that is committed to sustainability at its core. I look forward to working with the talented team at [Fusion Park](https://fusionpark.us/) to drive meaningful change and make a positive impact on the environment. Our mission is clear: to create innovative, sustainable solutions that benefit not only our company but also the planet to inclusively explore Growth with Sustainability”
