[Granules India Ltd.](https://granulesindia.com/), a global pharmaceutical manufacturer renowned for its commitment to quality and responsibility, has been awarded the prestigious EcoVadis Gold Medal. This places **Granules India among the top 5% of over 130,000 companies worldwide rated by EcoVadis** — the leading global standard for business sustainability ratings.

Granules India’s performance was exceptional across all four EcoVadis categories:

*   **Environment**: 78/100
*   **Labour & Human Rights**: 74/100
*   **Ethics**: 84/100
*   **Sustainable Procurement**: 83/100

This remarkable achievement reflects Granules India’s unwavering focus on Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) principles and was made possible through a structured sustainability roadmap developed in collaboration with Growlity, Inc. USA — a global ESG and sustainability consulting firm.

_[Mr. Sanjay Kumar](https://www.linkedin.com/in/sanjay-kumar-2b673714/), Chief Strategy & Sustainability Officer, Granules India Ltd. said:_

We appreciate Growlity support in translating our vision into an actionable framework, benchmarking our progress globally, and aligning with stakeholder expectations. They also helped us strengthen our disclosures during the assessment process.”_

_[Mr. Prabhat Pant](https://www.linkedin.com/in/prabhat-pant/), Vice President – Global Market Development, Growlity, added:

### **About Granules India Ltd.**

[Granules India](https://granulesindia.com/) is a globally integrated pharmaceutical company based in Hyderabad, India. It manufactures APIs, PFIs, and finished dosages for regulated and semi-regulated markets across more than 75 countries. With innovation, quality, and sustainability at its core, Granules India is committed to advancing healthcare through responsible business practices.
