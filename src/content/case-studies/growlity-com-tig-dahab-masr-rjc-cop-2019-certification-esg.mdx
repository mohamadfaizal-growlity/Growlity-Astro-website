Growlity Inc., a global consulting firm specializing in ESG, sustainability, and responsible business practices, is proud to announce its successful partnership with [TIG Dahab Masr](https://dahabmasr.com/), a leading gold trading company based in Cairo, Egypt, in achieving [Responsible Jewellery Council (RJC) Code of Practices (COP) 2019 Certification](https://www.responsiblejewellery.com/membership/find-an-rjc-member/?bycountry=Egypt&rjccategories=&rjccertification=&membertype=&sortby=countrydescending&searchbox=&pagenum=1). This milestone not only validates TIG Dahab Masr’ commitment to responsible sourcing and ethical business conduct but also positions the company on a progressive path toward ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) alignment and sustainability integration.

The [Responsible Jewellery Council (RJC)](https://growlity.com/solution/rjc-certification) is the world’s leading standards-setting organization for the global jewelry and watch industry. Its Code of Practices (COP) 2019 provides a framework for companies to demonstrate their commitment to responsible business practices, covering areas such as human rights, labor rights, environmental stewardship, product integrity, and responsible sourcing of gold, diamonds, and other precious materials. Achieving RJC certification is a globally recognized benchmark for ethical, transparent, and sustainable business operations.
