Growlity, a leading global [ESG & Sustainability consulting](https://www.growlity.com/consulting/) firm, is pleased to announce its instrumental role in successfully assisting [Waaree Energies Limited](https://waaree.com/), one of India’s premier renewable energy companies, in securing the prestigious [EcoVadis Gold Medal](https://www.growlity.com/ecovadis-assessment/) for Sustainability. This remarkable achievement underscores Waaree’s unwavering commitment to responsible business practices and adherence to the highest global ESG standards.

Waaree Energies Limited, a SEBI-listed company, embarked on a transformative sustainability journey, with Growlity serving as a trusted advisor. Our collaboration was marked by meticulous efforts in structuring robust ESG frameworks, aligning sustainability strategies with international best practices, and ensuring regulatory compliance.

### **Strategic ESG Advisory & Comprehensive Support**

Growlity’s expert team provided strategic guidance to Waaree Energies Limited by:

*   Developing and refining comprehensive ESG policies, ensuring alignment with global sustainability benchmarks.
*   Establishing and tracking key performance indicators (KPIs) to measure and enhance sustainability impact.
*   Conducting precise GHG emissions calculations, including Scope 1, 2, and 3 categories, ensuring accurate carbon footprint assessment.
*   Assisting in obtaining high-impact endorsements such as the United Nations Global Compact (UNGC) and the Science-Based Targets initiative (SBTi), reinforcing Waaree’s commitment to climate action.

This recognition is a testament to the proactive and collaborative efforts between Growlity and Waaree’s sustainability team, setting a benchmark for corporate ESG leadership in the renewable energy sector.
