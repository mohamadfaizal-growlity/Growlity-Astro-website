[Growlity](https://www.growlity.com/about-us/), a global leader in ESG and business sustainability management advisory services, proudly announces the launch of its newly designed office space at Surat, India, based on [green building concepts](https://www.growlity.com/green-building-certification/), reflecting the company’s unwavering commitment to environmental responsibility, innovation, and employee well-being. The office embodies modern sustainability practices through its use of green-certified and eco-friendly materials across all aspects of construction and design.

### **A Step Towards a Greener Future**

Growlity new office is a testament of our dedication to reducing our [greenhouse gas (carbon) footprint](https://www.growlity.com/consulting/). Every element of the workspace has been thoughtfully designed to promote sustainability, energy efficiency, and employee well-being. From construction materials to daily operations, our green office sets new standards in corporate environmental stewardship. The new Growlity office is a manifestation of sustainable interior design, featuring eco-friendly materials, energy-efficient solutions, and a biophilic design that seamlessly integrates nature into the workspace.

### **Sustainable Building Materials**

From responsibly sourced and workstation furniture to low-VOC paints to GreenPro certified carpets and recycled materials, every element of the office has been carefully chosen to minimize environmental impact while fostering a healthy and productive work environment. Our office is constructed using environmentally friendly and sustainable materials, ensuring a minimal ecological impact.

![growlity-new-office](https://www.growlity.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/growlity-new-office-13.png "growlity-new-office (13)")

![growlity-new-office](https://www.growlity.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/growlity-new-office-4.png "growlity-new-office (4)")

### **Energy Efficiency & Natural Lighting for Enhanced Well-being**

With Green building concepts architectural design, our office maximizes natural lighting, minimizing the need for artificial illumination during the day time, which has resulted to reduce around 70% of our electricity consumption as compared to other conventional offices. Additionally, energy-efficient 5 star rated appliances, air conditioning systems, and cutting edge technology in LED lighting and fans, internet and surveillance systems have been incorporated to optimize power usage and promote sustainability while maintaining a comfortable working environment.

![growlity-new-office](https://www.growlity.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/growlity-new-office-6.png "growlity-new-office (6)")

![growlity-new-office](https://www.growlity.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/growlity-new-office-5.png "growlity-new-office (5)")

### **Water & Waste Management**

We have incorporated sustainable water fixtures that enhance efficiency and significantly reduce water waste. Additionally, from the beginning of this office we have implemented zero waste management system that emphasizes recycling and responsible disposal of materials.

### **Paperless Office**

Since our inception in 2020, Growlity operates as a paperless company, eliminating the use of paper in all our operations globally. We never practice to give or receive paper based visiting cards. Our team members never use even paper based diary in day to day work. By leveraging digital solutions, we enhance efficiency, reduce waste, and contribute to environmental conservation. Our commitment to a paperless workplace aligns with our broader sustainability goals, reinforcing our dedication to a greener future.

![growlity-new-office](https://www.growlity.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/growlity-new-office-11.png "growlity-new-office (11)")

![growlity-new-office](https://www.growlity.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/growlity-new-office-12.png "growlity-new-office (12)")

### **Raw & Minimalist Ceiling Design**

As part of our sustainable design approach, we have chosen to keep the ceiling raw, exposing the concrete. This not only reduces the use of additional materials but also enhances the industrial aesthetic of the workspace, promoting a unique and eco-conscious architectural design.

![growlity-new-office](https://www.growlity.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/growlity-office-ceiling.png "growlity-office-ceiling")

### **A Collaborative and Dynamic Team Environment**

Growlity’s office layout has been thoughtfully designed to encourage collaboration, innovation, and teamwork. Open workspaces, creative brainstorming zones, and dedicated sustainability research areas enable team members to share ideas and drive impactful solutions. Ergonomic seating, breakout spaces, cafeteria, pantry, game zone and greenery-infused workstations further enhance comfort and efficiency, making it an ideal setting for sustainable business growth.
