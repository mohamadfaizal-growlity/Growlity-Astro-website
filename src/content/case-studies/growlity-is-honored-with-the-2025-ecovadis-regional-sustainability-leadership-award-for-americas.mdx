[Growlity Inc.](https://www.growlity.com/) is proud to announce that we have been recognized for excellence in sustainability performance and have been named as the winner of the **2025 EcoVadis Regional Sustainability Leadership Award (Americas).** This prestigious recognition is awarded to a select group of small- and medium-sized companies worldwide that have demonstrated outstanding commitment to sustainability, achieving exceptionally high performance in their 2024 EcoVadis assessment.

![ecovadis-platinum-consultant-training-partner-americas](https://www.growlity.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/ecovadis-platinum-consultant-trainingpartner.png "ecovadis-platinum-consultant-training-partner")

[EcoVadis](https://ecovadis.com/), a globally renowned sustainability ratings provider, has acknowledged [Growlity Inc.’s](https://www.growlity.com/) continuous efforts in driving impactful environmental, social, and governance (ESG) initiatives. This award reinforces our dedication to integrating sustainability into our business operations and supporting organizations worldwide in their sustainability journeys.

We are ecovadis approved consultant and training partner since 2023.

The announcement of the 2025 EcoVadis Regional Sustainability Leadership Award winners has taken place on **Day 1 of Sustain, EcoVadis’ annual flagship event in Paris, on March 11, 2025**. [Sustain](https://resources.ecovadis.com/sustain-conference) is a premier global event bringing together industry leaders, sustainability professionals, and key stakeholders to advance the conversation on sustainable business practices.

![growlity-ecovadis-score-card](https://www.growlity.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/growlity-ecovadis-score-card.png "growlity-ecovadis-score-card")

Securing not only a Platinum Medal and ranking in the top 1% globally but with an impressive overall score of 95/100. This accomplishment is a testament to our unwavering commitment to ESG & Sustainability Management across all key themes—Environment (100/100), Labor & Human Rights (90/100) and Ethics (100/100). Our dedication to responsible business practices, transparency, and sustainable impact continues to drive us forward, setting a benchmark for excellence in corporate sustainability. This recognition reinforces our mission to lead by example and help organizations worldwide in their sustainability journeys.

![dr-nitin-dumasia-president-and-ceo-growlity](https://www.growlity.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/dr-nitin-dumasia-new-office.png "dr-nitin-dumasia")

_“We are honored to receive this recognition from EcoVadis,” said [Dr. Nitin Dumasia,](https://www.linkedin.com/in/dr-nitin-dumasia-b6182616/) President & CEO of Growlity Inc. “This award is a testament to our relentless commitment to ESG excellence, innovation, and responsible business practices. We are dedicated to helping businesses worldwide achieve their sustainability goals and create a positive impact.”_

_“This recognition from EcoVadis is a reflection of our unwavering commitment to driving sustainability excellence globally,” said [Mr. Prabhat Pant,](https://www.linkedin.com/in/prabhat-pant/) Vice President, Global Market Development & Partnerships. “At Growlity, we believe in empowering businesses with the right strategies and solutions to accelerate their ESG journey. This award further motivates us to continue our mission of fostering responsible and sustainable business growth.”_

![mr-prabhat-pant-vice-president-growlity](https://www.growlity.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/mr-prabhat-pant-vice-president-growlity.png "mr-prabhat-pant-vice-president-growlity")

We extend our heartfelt gratitude to [EcoVadis](https://ecovadis.com/) for this honor and to [our clients](https://www.growlity.com/our-clients/), partners, and [team members](https://www.growlity.com/about-us/) who have been instrumental in our journey toward sustainability excellence.
