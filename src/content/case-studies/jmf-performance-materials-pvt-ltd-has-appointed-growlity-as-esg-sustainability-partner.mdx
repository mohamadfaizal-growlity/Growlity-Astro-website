Mumbai, November 30, 2023

[JMF Performance Materials](https://jmfindia.com/), is a leading distributor of hi-tech synthetic rubbers and chemicals sourced from Japan, is thrilled to announce its strategic collaboration with Growlity, a renowned firm specializing in Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) & Sustainability solutions. This partnership marks a pivotal step towards enhancing [JMF Performance’s](https://jmfindia.com/) commitment to sustainable practices and fostering a culture of responsible business operations.

In an era where corporate responsibility takes center stage, [JMF Performance](https://jmfindia.com/) recognizes the critical importance of aligning its operations with ESG principles. With Growlity onboard as their [ESG & Sustainability Partner](https://www.growlity.com/consulting/), [JMF Performance](https://jmfindia.com/) aims to leverage expertise, guidance, and innovative strategies to advance their sustainability objectives.

“At [JMF Performance](https://jmfindia.com/), we are dedicated to not only delivering excellence in our products/services but also ensuring that our operations align with sustainable practices,” “We are excited to collaborate with Growlity, whose team of experts will enable us to navigate the complex landscape of [ESG and sustainability](https://www.growlity.com/consulting/) effectively” said [Mr. Vishal Jhunjhunwala](https://www.linkedin.com/in/vishal-jhunjhunwala-5301a0191/), Director of JMF Performance Materials Pvt. Ltd.

One of the primary focuses of this partnership is to meticulously calculate [JMF Performance’s](https://jmfindia.com/) Greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions. [Growlity’s proficient team of domain experts](https://www.growlity.com/) will work closely with [JMF Performance](https://jmfindia.com/) to derive precise GHG emission calculations, enabling the company to strategize reduction measures effectively.

Moreover, Growlity’s tailored [training programs](https://www.growlity.com/training/) on various facets of ESG and sustainability will empower [JMF Performance’s](https://jmfindia.com/) workforce. These training modules aim to educate and engage employees, instilling a culture of sustainability and embedding responsible practices into the core of the company’s operations.

In addition to internal initiatives, the partnership will extend efforts towards engaging stakeholders. By collaborating with [Growlity](https://www.growlity.com/about-us/), JMF Performance intends to foster dialogue and collaboration with stakeholders, ensuring alignment and transparency in sustainability goals.

The commitment to renewable energy sources is another crucial aspect of this collaboration. Growlity will advise JMF Performance on adopting renewable energy solutions, aligning the company’s energy consumption with sustainable alternatives.

Furthermore, the partnership will provide exclusive assistance in undertaking the [EcoVadis Sustainability Assessment](https://www.growlity.com/ecovadis-assessment/). This assessment will enable JMF Performance to evaluate its sustainability performance comprehensively, identifying strengths and areas for improvement to enhance its overall ESG standing.

As a result of this collaboration, JMF Performance anticipates making significant strides towards a more sustainable future. The partnership with Growlity underscores JMF Performance’s dedication to pioneering sustainable practices within their industry.
