Mumbai, India, December 15, 2023

[Ketan Brothers Diamonds Exports](http://www.ketanbrothers.com/default.aspx), based in Mumbai, India, proudly presents its inaugural Sustainability Report for the fiscal year 2022-23. This milestone publication reflects the company’s commitment to transparency, ethical practices, and environmental responsibility. The report signifies Ketan Brothers’ proactive stance in fostering sustainability within the diamond trading sector.

The release of the Sustainability Report coincides with the company’s strategic partnership with Growlity, a reputable and global firm specializing in [Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) strategies](https://www.growlity.com/strategy/) and Sustainability solutions. Growlity’s expertise has been instrumental in aiding Ketan Brothers Diamondz Exports in calculating their greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions and devising strategies to offset their carbon footprint, mitigating their environmental impact.

Partner of Ketan Brothers Diamonds Exports, Mr. Jay Parikh, expressed warmth about this milestone, stating, “Our inaugural Sustainability Report represents a pivotal step in our journey toward sustainable and responsible business practices. Collaborating with [Growlity](https://www.linkedin.com/company/growlity-inc/) has been instrumental in assessing our environmental impact and taking proactive measures to reduce it.”

Moreover, President & CEO of Growlity, [Dr. Nitin Dumasia](https://www.linkedin.com/in/dr-nitin-dumasia-b6182616/), highlighted the significance of this partnership, saying, “Partnering with Ketan Brothers Diamondz Exports signifies a shared commitment to promoting sustainability within the diamond trading industry. Our collaboration has not only facilitated the measurement of GHG emissions but has also allowed us to explore effective strategies to mitigate environmental impact.”

The partnership between [Ketan Brothers Diamondz Exports](https://www.linkedin.com/company/ketan-brothers-diamond-exports/about/) and [Growlity](https://www.linkedin.com/company/growlity-inc/) underscores a joint commitment to integrate sustainability as a core component of operations within the natural diamond trading sector, driving positive change and responsible practices.
