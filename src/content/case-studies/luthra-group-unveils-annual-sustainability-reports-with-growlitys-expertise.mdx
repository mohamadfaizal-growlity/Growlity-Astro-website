Growlity is proud to announce its successful collaboration with [Luthra Group](https://www.luthraindia.com/) in the publication of their Annual Sustainability Report for FY 2023-24, marking the three consecutive years of partnership in sustainability reporting. This milestone further solidifies Growlity’s expertise in [ESG and sustainability consulting](https://www.growlity.com/consulting/) and advisory, ensuring that Luthra Group’s reporting aligns with [Global Reporting Initiative (GRI) Standards](https://www.globalreporting.org/) while maintaining a visually compelling and insightful presentation of their ESG journey and impact for circular economy business.

![luthra-group-annual-sustainability-report-unvieling](https://www.growlity.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/luthra-group-annual-sustainability-report-unvieling-2.png "luthra-group-annual-sustainability-report-unvieling (2)")

The unveiling of Luthra Group’s latest Sustainability and ESG Report took place during their 54th National Safety Week celebrations on 7th March 2025, an event that underscored the organization’s dedication to safety, environmental compliance, and responsible business practices. The event was attended by esteemed dignitaries, including Shri R.A. Patel, Joint Director of Industrial Safety and Health, Surat, and [Dr. Jignasa Oza](https://www.linkedin.com/in/dr-jignasa-chhaya-dishank-oza-a1022162/), Regional Officer, Gujarat Pollution Control Board, Surat.

[Dr. Nitin Dumasia](https://www.linkedin.com/in/dr-nitin-dumasia-b6182616/), President & CEO of Growlity Inc., was invited as a key guest at the event, where he shared valuable insights into the ESG framework, Net Zero strategies, and the evolving sustainability landscape. His address emphasized the growing importance of integrating ESG principles into corporate strategies to drive long-term value creation and compliance with global sustainability expectations.

![dr-nitin-dumasia-sharing-valuable-insghits-on-esg](https://www.growlity.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/dr-nitin-dumasia-sharing-valuable-insghits-on-esg.png "dr-nitin-dumasia-sharing-valuable-insghits-on-esg")

![luthra-group-annual-sustainability-reports](https://www.growlity.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/luthra-group-annual-sustainability-reports.png "luthra-group-annual-sustainability-reports")

The 2023-24 report, along with the previous FY 2021-22 and FY 2022-23 reports, has evolved through a collaborative effort between Growlity’s domain experts and Luthra Group’s internal team. Our role extended beyond technical reporting—we provided strategic guidance on sustainability goals, data structuring, and impactful storytelling to enhance stakeholder engagement. The final outcome is a testament to Luthra Group’s unwavering commitment to transparency, sustainable development, and corporate responsibility.
