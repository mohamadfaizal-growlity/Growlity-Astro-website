[PK Enterprises](http://permeshwarimages.com/), a leader in world-class high-fashion garments and textile products, has reached a milestone that many organizations strive for over years of dedicated effort. In **2026**, the company was awarded the **prestigious EcoVadis Gold Medal**—a distinction achieved by **less than 5% of the more than 150,000+ companies evaluated globally**.

This remarkable accomplishment places [PK Enterprises](http://permeshwarimages.com/) among the top 5% of companies globally, with an EcoVadis score of **82**, demonstrating leadership in transparency, accountability, and responsible business practices in the garment and textile industry.

The achievement reflects PK Enterprises’ **early strategic vision, disciplined execution, and genuine commitment to embedding sustainability at the core of its operations**. By integrating ESG principles into every aspect of its business, [PK Enterprises](<http://PK Enterprises>) continues to raise the bar for responsible innovation and sustainable excellence across the global textile value chain.

## ****Outstanding Performance Across All Sustainability Pillars****

[PK Enterprises](http://permeshwarimages.com/) delivered exceptional scores across all four EcoVadis assessment pillars:

*   🌿 **Environment:** 81/100
*   👥 **Labor & Human Rights:**   84/100
*   ⚖️ **Ethics:** 79/100
*   🔗 **Sustainable Procurement:** 81/100

**📊 Overall Score:** 82 / 100  
**🏅 Global Percentile Ranking:** 97th (Top 5% Worldwide)

This achievement is particularly significant for a company operating in the textile sector, where sustainability leadership requires innovation not just in products, but across the entire value chain — from responsible material sourcing to garment manufacturing and end-of-life considerations.

PK Enterprises’ historic achievement was made possible through its strategic collaboration with [**Growlity Private Ltd**,](https://growlity.com/) a premier ESG and sustainability consulting firm with proven expertise in guiding organizations to global ESG excellence.
