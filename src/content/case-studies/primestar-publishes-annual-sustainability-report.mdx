[_Primestar_,](https://primestardiamonds.com/) a leading name in the diamond industry, has officially released its **Annual Sustainability Report**, reflecting the company’s strong commitment to ethical practices, climate action, and sustainable business growth.

The report highlights Primestar’ journey towards **responsible sourcing, reducing carbon footprint, transparent ESG disclosures, and community empowerment initiatives.** With sustainability at the core of its operations, the company continues to set new benchmarks for the diamond industry by balancing profitability with purpose.

![](https://growlity.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/08/Mr.-Pranav-Kothari-Director-Primestar.png)

## **Message From Primestar**

_At Primestar, sustainability is more than a responsibility – it is our way forward. This report is not just about what we achieved, but how we are shaping a future where business and sustainability go hand in hand,”_ said  
_[Mr. Pranav Kothari, Director, Primestar.](https://www.linkedin.com/in/kotharipranav/?originalSubdomain=be)_

The Annual Sustainability Report covers key focus areas including:

*   **Environmental Stewardship:** Initiatives for energy efficiency, waste management, and lowering greenhouse gas emissions.
*   **Responsible Sourcing & Supply Chain:** Strengthening transparency across the diamond value chain with ethical sourcing practices.
*   **Social Responsibility:** Investments in employee well-being, diversity, inclusion, and community development.

This milestone not only reflects Primestar’ sustainability journey but also resonates with its vision of achieving **“Growth with Sustainability”** – a philosophy shared by Growlity, the advisory partner behind the report.
