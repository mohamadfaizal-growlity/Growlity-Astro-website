Thane, Maharashtra, India – August 06, 2024

[Raymond Realty](https://raymondrealty.in/), the real estate arm of the renowned Raymond Group, proudly announces its on-going commitment to sustainability and ethical practices through a strategic partnership with [Growlity](https://www.growlity.com/). This collaboration marks a significant step towards reinforcing [Raymond Realty’s](https://raymondrealty.in/) commitment to Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) principles and sustainable business practices.

Since its inception in 1925, Raymond has been synonymous with trust, quality, and excellence, consistently raising the bar in every industry domain it ventures into, be it fashion, lifestyle, technology, or real estate. Raymond Realty, building upon the group’s rich legacy, is focused on setting new benchmarks in the real estate industry with customer centricity at its core. The collaboration with Growlity underscores Raymond Realty’s dedication to pushing the envelope on construction quality, design aesthetic, and comfort feasibility, ensuring a living experience unlike any other.

![](https://www.growlity.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/08/1.png "1")

[Mr Sandeep Maheshwari](https://www.linkedin.com/in/sandeep-maheshwari-0348566a/), Chief Operating Officer of [Raymond Realty](https://raymondrealty.in/) conveyed his enthusiasm for the on-going sustainability initiatives, stating, “At Raymond Realty, we understand the crucial role that businesses play in shaping a sustainable future. Our partnership with [Growlity](https://www.growlity.com/) to enhance our ESG practices highlights our dedication to responsible business operations. We believe that strong corporate governance, community development, and biodiversity preservation are essential components of our journey towards a greener and more sustainable world.”

The collaboration with Growlity will cover multiple aspects of sustainability, with a strong emphasis on enhancing [Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) practices.](https://www.growlity.com/strategy/) Growlity’s team of [experienced experts](https://www.growlity.com/about-us/) will partner closely with Raymond Realty to accurately assess ESG metrics, including precise calculations of greenhouse gas emissions, thereby laying the groundwork for effective strategies to reduce environmental impact and improve overall sustainability performance.

![](https://www.growlity.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/08/Core-Team.png "Core Team")

Additionally, Growlity will conduct comprehensive [training sessions](https://www.growlity.com/training/) covering diverse topics of ESG and sustainability. These specialized training modules aim to equip Raymond Realty’s workforce with the knowledge and tools necessary to integrate sustainable practices effectively into their day-to-day operations.

![](https://www.growlity.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/08/2.png "2")

[Dr. Nitin Dumasia](https://www.linkedin.com/in/dr-nitin-dumasia-b6182616/), President & CEO of [Growlity](https://www.growlity.com/) expressed his commitment to supporting [Raymond Realty](https://raymondrealty.in/) in achieving its sustainability goals. “We are honoured to collaborate with Raymond Realty on their sustainability journey. Our partnership focuses on a comprehensive range of initiatives, including GHG emissions calculations, community development, and fostering robust corporate governance. Together, we aim to create a positive and lasting impact on the environment and society, and future generations by promoting sustainable living and responsible business practices.”

The partnership signifies a concerted effort to align Raymond Realty’s operations with global best practices in sustainability. By integrating ESG principles into its business strategy, Raymond Realty aims to contribute to a more sustainable and resilient urban ecosystem, offering living spaces that not only provide comfort and elegance but also prioritize environmental responsibility.
