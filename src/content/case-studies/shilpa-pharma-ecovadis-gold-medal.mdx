## About Shilpa Pharma Pvt. Ltd.

Shilpa Pharma Pvt. Ltd. is a leading pharmaceutical CDMO and API manufacturer established in 1987. With three manufacturing sites and a workforce of 1500+, including 100+ R&D specialists, the company serves 500+ global customers. Shilpa Pharma delivers high-quality products and services across NCE development, clinical supply, commercial manufacturing, and analytical services.

The company’s operations are aligned with USFDA standards, UNGC principles, SDGs, PSCI guidelines, NDC, and Science-Based Targets Initiative (SBTi), reinforcing its commitment to long-term sustainability.
