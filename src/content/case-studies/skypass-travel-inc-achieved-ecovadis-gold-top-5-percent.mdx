[Skypass Travel Inc (Group),](https://www.skypasstravel.com/) a growing and dynamic travel solutions provider, has proudly achieved the Gold Medal **(Feb 2025)** from EcoVadis for its **Group-level** corporate sustainability performance. This significant milestone places Skypass among the top-performing companies globally assessed by EcoVadis—a recognition that is both prestigious and challenging to achieve, particularly within a network of over **150,000+ companies** evaluated across more than **180+ countries** and **220+ industry** categories.

Receiving the **Gold Medal** at the **Group level** reflects years of sustained effort, strategic execution, and continuous improvement integrated across all subsidiaries and operational entities under the Skypass Travel Inc (Group) structure.

## **Measured Performance Across Sustainability Indicators.**

*   🌿 **Environment:** 78/100
*   👥 **Labor & Human Rights:**   78/100
*   ⚖️ **Ethics:** 81/100
*   🔗 **Sustainable Procurement:** 81/100

**📊 Overall Score:** 79/ 100  
**🏅 Global Percentile Ranking:** 95th (Top 5% Worldwide)

These outstanding results underscore **Skypass** position as a sustainability-focused organization within the global travel and tourism sector. Achieving this level of recognition requires not only strong governance and compliance, but also a genuine culture of environmental responsibility, ethical business conduct, and continuous improvement aligned with the rigorous standards of EcoVadis.

## ****🚀 A Sustainability Journey That Inspires Confidence and Pride****

This milestone reflects a strong transformation at Skypass Travel Inc (Group), where sustainability has grown from an initiative into a core strength of the organization. In a changing global environment—marked by regulatory shifts, climate risks, and evolving stakeholder expectations—Skypass has responded proactively, turning challenges into opportunities for improvement. ESG principles are now integrated into daily decision-making, shaping governance, responsible procurement, environmental management, climate action, and ethical business practices.

The journey was not without its challenges. The Group navigated **complex data consolidation across entities, evolving regulatory expectations, rigorous documentation requirements, and detailed evidence validation** during the assessment process. Each challenge became an opportunity to strengthen internal systems, enhance cross-functional collaboration, and build greater transparency and accountability across operations.

Earning the Gold Medal from EcoVadis is more than recognition; it validates Skypass’s continued commitment to integrity, accountability, and improvement. It reinforces that sustainability is not just an initiative, but a central part of how the organization operates and grows
