Arizona, USA, January 16, 2023

Specialty Sign Co., a prominent force in the signage printing industry based in Arizona, USA, proudly announces a strategic appointment of [Growlity](https://www.growlity.com/), a leading sustainability advisory firm and an [Approved Consultant & Training Partner from EcoVadis](https://www.growlity.com/ecovadis-assessment/). This partnership signals a pioneering effort in advancing sustainability within the signage printing sector.

The alliance between Specialty Sign Co. and Growlity signifies a progressive step towards integrating EcoVadis sustainability assessments into the core of signage printing operations. By harnessing Growlity’s expertise, Specialty Sign Co. aims to heighten its commitment to eco-conscious practices, striving for enhanced environmental responsibility and ethical business conduct within the signage industry.

_Founder of Specialty Sign Co., Mr. Tim Floyd_, expressed excitement about the collaboration, stating, “Specialty Sign Co. has always been dedicated to delivering innovative solutions while prioritizing environmental consciousness. Teaming up with Growlity aligns perfectly with our commitment to further elevate sustainability in our operations and contribute positively to our industry.”

_As an Approved EcoVadis Training Partner & Consultant at Growlity, [Mr. Shwetang Kapadia](https://www.linkedin.com/in/shwetang-kapadia-26b01a99/)_ emphasized the significance of this collaboration, stating, “In the signage printing business, sustainability is pivotal. Our partnership with Specialty Sign Co. marks an exciting journey to integrate [EcoVadis assessments](https://www.growlity.com/ecovadis-assessment/), ensuring responsible sourcing, production, and operations, thereby shaping a more sustainable future for the signage industry.”

This collaboration underscores the shared vision of Specialty Sign Co. and Growlity to spearhead sustainability as a cornerstone in signage printing, fostering innovation, and positive environmental impact.
