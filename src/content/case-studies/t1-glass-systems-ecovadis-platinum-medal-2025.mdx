## ******Joining the World’s Most Exclusive Sustainability Elite on First Attempt******

[T1 Glass Systems Pte Ltd,](https://www.t1glass.com/) a leading innovator in acoustic glazed partition systems solution, has achieved what most companies spend years pursuing: the prestigious **EcoVadis Platinum Medal** — an honor bestowed upon **less than 1% of over 130,000 assessed companies worldwide**. This extraordinary milestone, achieved in **2025**, places T1 Glass among an **exceptionally exclusive group** — fewer than **1,300 companies** out of the entire global assessment pool hold this distinction. What makes this achievement even more remarkable is that T1 Glass earned Platinum scoring of 95 in their inaugural EcoVadis assessment, places T1 Glass among the most sustainable companies globally — setting a new benchmark for transparency and accountability in the architectural glass and acoustic partition industry.

T1 Glass Systems joins this circle with a result that highlights the company’s early vision, disciplined approach, and authentic commitment to building sustainability into the way they operate.

## ****Outstanding Performance Across All Sustainability Pillars****

T1 Glass Systems delivered exceptional scores across all four EcoVadis assessment pillars:

**Environment:** 100 / 100

**Labor & Human Rights:** 98 / 100

**Ethics:** 73 / 100

**Sustainable Procurement:** 97 / 100

**Overall Score:** 95 / 100

**Global Percentile Ranking:** 99+ (Top 1% Worldwide)

This achievement is particularly significant for a company operating in the construction and interior solutions sector, where sustainability leadership requires innovation not just in products, but across the entire value chain — from responsible material sourcing to installation practices and end-of-life considerations.
