### [**About Tairui Electronics Group**](http://www.tairuie.com/index.php?c=category&id=1)

[Tairui Electronics Group](http://www.tairuie.com/index.php?c=category&id=1) has two R&D centers in Shenzhen, two national production bases (located in Shenzhen and Dongguan respectively), and overseas production bases in Mexico, the United States, and Malaysia. Its products are exported to more than 20 countries and regions worldwide, making it one of the representative enterprises of China’s “Intelligent Manufacturing” going global.

### **About E&L Lighting**

E&L Lighting, based in Wisconsin, USA, is a prominent lighting solutions provider known for its high-quality, energy-efficient products. As a unit of Tairui Electronics Group, the company combines global innovation with local excellence.
