[Transpek Industries Limited](https://transpek.com/), a globally trusted name in chemical manufacturing and a publicly-listed company on India’s capital makets, has been awarded the [EcoVadis Gold Medal](https://support.ecovadis.com/hc/en-us/articles/210460227-Understanding-EcoVadis-Medals-and-Badges), marking a significant achievement in its sustainability journey. This distinction places Transpek among the top 5% of companies globally assessed by EcoVadis, the world’s leading sustainability ratings platform.

This major milestone reflects Transpek’s deep-rooted commitment to environmental stewardship, responsible business ethics, and sustainable supply chain practices. The achievement was made possible with the strategic guidance of [Growlity, Inc. USA](https://www.growlity.com/), a global leader in ESG advisory, sustainability consulting, and EcoVadis strategy.

Being a publicly listed company on India’s capital markets, Transpek’s achievement holds even greater significance. The EcoVadis Gold Medal underscores Transpek’s proactive approach to transparency, risk management, and long-term value creation for all stakeholders, including investors.

### **About Transpek**

[Established in 1965](https://transpek.com/milestones-accolades/), Transpek Industries Ltd. specializes in manufacturing chlorinated chemicals, supplying to over 60 countries. Known for its innovation, quality, and ethical practices, Transpek serves diverse sectors including pharmaceuticals, agrochemicals, polymers, and more. Sustainability is a cornerstone of its business strategy, driving positive environmental and social impact across operations.
