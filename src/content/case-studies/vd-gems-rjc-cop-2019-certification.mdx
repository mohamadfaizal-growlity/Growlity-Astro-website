[V. D. Gems Limited](https://www.linkedin.com/company/vdgemsltd/), one of Hong Kong’s trusted diamond companies, has proudly achieved the [Responsible Jewellery Council (RJC) Code of Practices (COP) 2019 Certification.](https://www.responsiblejewellery.com/wp-content/uploads/V-D-Gems-Limited-RJC-Certificate-COP-2025-EU.pdf) This milestone was reached with expert support and guidance from Growlity Inc., a global consulting firm that helps companies strengthen their [ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance)](https://growlity.com/solution/esg-assessment) and sustainability practices.

This certification highlights _V. D. Gems’_ strong commitment to doing business with integrity, transparency, and responsibility. It also marks an important step in their journey toward becoming a more sustainable and trusted name in the diamond industry.

## ******V. D. Gems’ Journey Toward Responsible Practices******

Sharing his thoughts on this achievement, [Mr. Dharmesh Vaghani, Director of V. D. Gems Limited,](https://www.linkedin.com/in/dharmeshvaghani88/) said:

![](https://growlity.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/mr-dharmesh-vaghani-vd-gems-01.png)

_“We are extremely proud to achieve the RJC COP 2019 Certification. It shows our dedication to responsible and sustainable practices in everything we do. The Growlity team guided us at every step — from setting up systems to meeting the standards. This certification gives more confidence to our customers and partners, showing that we care about people, the planet, and ethical business values.”_

With this achievement, _V. D. Gems Limited_ has proven its focus on **ethical sourcing, safe workplaces, fair labor practices, and environmental care** — all key elements of the RJC Code of Practices.

## ******Growlity’s Role in the Success******

[Dr. Nitin Dumasia, President & CEO of Growlity Inc.](https://www.linkedin.com/in/dr-nitin-dumasia-b6182616/), shared his appreciation for the collaboration:

![](https://growlity.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/dr-nitin-dumasia-vd-gems-1.png)

_“It was a pleasure working with the V. D. Gems team. Their leadership was open to learning and adopting best practices in sustainability and governance. Our role was to help them not just meet the RJC COP 2019 requirements but also build a stronger ESG foundation for their future growth.”_

Growlity supported V. D. Gems throughout the process — from assessing gaps and preparing documentation to training teams and improving internal policies — ensuring that the company was fully ready for the RJC audit and certification.
