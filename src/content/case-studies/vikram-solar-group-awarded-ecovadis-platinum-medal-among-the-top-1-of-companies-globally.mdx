[The Vikram Solar Group](https://www.vikramsolar.com/), a leading global solar energy solutions provider, has been awarded the prestigious EcoVadis Platinum Medal, placing it in the top 1% of over 130,000 companies rated worldwide by EcoVadis — the leading global standard for business sustainability ratings.

The Platinum Medal demonstrates the Group’s steadfast commitment to Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) principles. This achievement was made possible through a comprehensive and structured ESG transformation journey, developed in collaboration with [Growlity, a leading EcoVadis consultant](https://www.growlity.com/ecovadis-assessment/) and a globally recognized ESG and sustainability advisory firm.

_[Mr. Gyanesh Chaudhary](https://www.linkedin.com/in/gyaneshc/), Chairman and Managing Director, Vikram Solar Group, said:_  
_“This recognition is a reflection of our long-standing belief that sustainability is not a parallel goal but a core business imperative. At Vikram Solar, we strive towards embedding responsible practices across our value chain — from sourcing to delivery — and have been consistently working towards becoming a future-ready, ethical manufacturer. Being rated among the top 1% at a globally recognised platform reaffirms our commitment to creating long-term value for our stakeholders while driving positive impact for the planet.”_

_[Dr. Nitin Dumasia](https://www.linkedin.com/in/dr-nitin-dumasia-b6182616/), Founder and CEO, Growlity Private Limited, added:_  
_“The Vikram Solar Group’s ESG journey is a benchmark for excellence in the renewable energy sector. Their leadership, focus, and operational integration of sustainability principles were commendable. We are proud to have supported them in achieving this globally significant milestone.”_

### **About Vikram Solar Group**

[Vikram Solar Limited](https://www.vikramsolar.com/) is one of the leading Indian solar PV module manufacturers, specializing in high efficiency photovoltaic (PV) module manufacturing, with an international presence across 39 countries as of Fiscal 2024. Headquartered in Kolkata, West Bengal, it is one of the largest PV module manufacturers in India with cumulative production capacity of 4.5 GW in Fiscal 2025. Vikram Solar is a ‘Top Performer’ in PVEL’s PV module reliability scorecard in 2024 and has been included in the Tier 1 solar PV modules manufacturer list of Bloomberg NEF as of the first three quarters of CY 2024.
