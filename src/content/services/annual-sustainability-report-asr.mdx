## What is the S&P ESG Score?

### What is ASR in ESG?

ASR stands for Annual Sustainability Report, a document that shares your company’s yearly ESG performance and commitments.

### Is an ASR mandatory?

In many countries, sustainability reporting is becoming mandatory—like BRSR in India or CSRD in Europe. Even when optional, an ASR boosts trust and competitiveness.

### Which ESG frameworks are covered in an ASR?

We align your ASR with GRI Standards, UN SDGs, SASB, TCFD, and BRSR depending on your goals and stakeholder needs.

### Can Growlity create the entire report from scratch?

Yes. From data collection and materiality assessment to writing, designing, and publishing—we manage the full ASR lifecycle.

### How often should a company publish an ASR?

Annually. A consistent year-on-year ASR shows long-term ESG commitment and progress tracking.
