## The B Corp Certification Process

We follow a 5-step process aligned with B Lab’s framework to help you achieve B Corp certification:  
**Discovery & Readiness** – We assess your eligibility and walk you through the B Impact Assessment (BIA).  
**Gap Analysis** – Estimate your score and identify areas for improvement.  
**Action Implementation** – Help you build and implement policies, systems, and practices.  
**B Lab Submission** – Finalize the BIA, submit it, and support you during the verification process.  
**Certification Achieved** – Celebrate your success and integrate B Corp into your ESG strategy.

### What is the cost of B Corp certification?

B Lab’s certification cost depends on annual revenue and ranges from $1,000 to $50,000+. We help you evaluate costs and maximize return on impact.

### How long does the certification take?

Typically 6–10 months, depending on company size and readiness. Growlity’s structured process accelerates your timeline.

### What is the B Impact Assessment (BIA)?

The BIA is a free, comprehensive tool to measure your company’s social and environmental impact. A minimum score of 80 out of 200 is required for certification.

### Can I get certified if I’m not yet ESG compliant?

Yes. Our consulting services bridge the gap between where you are and where you need to be, covering governance, employee well-being, carbon management, and more.

### Will I need to change my company’s legal status?

Some jurisdictions require adding benefit corporation clauses to your governance documents. We advise on this based on your location and company type.
