## What is ESG Assurance?

ESG Assurance is an independent verification process that confirms the accuracy, credibility, and completeness of your sustainability disclosures, whether it’s your Greenhouse Gas (GHG) inventory, Business Responsibility and Sustainability Report (BRSR), or Annual Sustainability Report (ASR).  
  
Using globally recognized standards like the AA1000 Standard (AA1000AS v3), third-party validates your data and builds stakeholder confidence. It also prepares your organization for regulatory scrutiny, investor evaluation, and global sustainability rankings. ### What are carbon credits and how do they work?

Carbon credits are permits that allow companies to offset emissions by funding verified climate projects.

### Why should businesses invest in carbon credits?

Investing in carbon credits helps meet net-zero targets, improve ESG scores, and boost brand reputation.

### What types of carbon offset projects are available?

Common projects include reforestation, renewable energy, clean cookstoves, and methane capture.

### How much does a carbon credit cost?

Prices vary by certification and market—from $5 to $50+ per ton in voluntary carbon markets.

### How can I buy or sell carbon credits?

Businesses can trade carbon credits via verified registries or marketplaces, depending on their sustainability goals.
