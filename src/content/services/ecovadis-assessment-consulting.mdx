## What Is EcoVadis Assessment?

The **[EcoVadis](https://ecovadis.com/) assessment** is a globally recognized sustainability rating system used by companies to evaluate suppliers and partners on environmental, social, and governance (ESG) performance.

The platform assesses companies across four key sustainability themes:

• Environment  
• Labor and Human Rights  
• Ethics  
• Sustainable Procurement

Organizations submit documentation, policies, and supporting evidence through the questionnaire. Based on the evaluation, companies receive a score and may qualify for medals such as Bronze, Silver, Gold, or Platinum.

Today, thousands of multinational companies rely on sustainability ratings to monitor supplier performance and ensure responsible sourcing practices.

Completing the process effectively allows businesses to:

• Strengthen supply chain relationships  
• Demonstrate ESG commitment  
• Meet buyer sustainability requirements  
• Improve transparency and risk management

## Understanding EcoVadis Score Levels and Medal Recognition

### How Scores and Medals Work

The EcoVadis assessment evaluates companies using a sustainability scoring system ranging from 0 to 100, based on performance across four ESG themes: Environment, Labor & Human Rights, Ethics, and Sustainable Procurement.

In addition to the score, companies may receive medals that recognize top-performing organizations relative to all companies evaluated globally.

Since 2024–2025, medals are awarded based on percentile ranking rather than fixed score thresholds. This means a company’s recognition depends on how it performs compared with other companies in the database over the previous 12 months.

**Current percentile recognition levels include:**

![Ecovadis assessment](https://growlity.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/platinum-2026.png)

#### Platinum Medal

Top 1% of companies

![Ecovadis assessment](https://growlity.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/gold-2026.png)

#### Silver Medal

![](https://growlity.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/bronze-2026.png)

#### Bronze Medal

Top 35% of companies

Companies outside these percentile ranges may still receive a **scorecard and improvement recommendations**, which can be used to strengthen sustainability performance in future assessments.  
  
This percentile approach ensures that sustainability recognition reflects **current global performance benchmarks rather than static thresholds.**

## EcoVadis Assessment Frequently Asked Questions

### What is an EcoVadis assessment?

+

An EcoVadis assessment is a sustainability evaluation system used by companies worldwide to measure the environmental, social, and ethical performance of businesses across global supply chains.

During the process, companies complete a structured questionnaire and submit supporting documentation that demonstrates their sustainability practices, policies, and management systems.

The assessment evaluates organizations across key ESG areas such as environmental impact, labor practices, business ethics, and responsible sourcing.

**Need help completing your assessment? [Book an EcoVadis Consultation →](https://growlity.com/contact-us)**

### What are the 4 pillars of EcoVadis?

+

The EcoVadis assessment framework evaluates companies across four sustainability pillars.

**Environment**  
Environmental management systems, carbon emissions, waste management, and climate initiatives.

**Labor and Human Rights**  
Employee safety, diversity, working conditions, and human rights protection.

**Ethics**  
Anti-corruption, governance, compliance, and responsible business conduct.

**Sustainable Procurement**  
Supplier sustainability management and responsible sourcing practices.

### How does the EcoVadis calculation work?

+

EcoVadis scores are calculated using a weighted assessment of sustainability policies, actions, and measurable results.

*   Policy commitments
*   Implementation actions
*   Performance indicators
*   Supporting evidence

Companies may receive Bronze, Silver, Gold, or Platinum recognition depending on final performance.

### Where can I find the EcoVadis questionnaire?

+

The EcoVadis questionnaire becomes available after registration on the EcoVadis platform.

The questionnaire is customized based on company size, industry, and operational geography.

Companies must provide sustainability policies, certifications, and supporting ESG documentation.

### What kind of questions are included in an EcoVadis assessment?

+

EcoVadis assessment questions focus on sustainability management practices and ESG performance.

*   Environmental management
*   Carbon reduction initiatives
*   Employee health and safety
*   Human rights practices
*   Anti-corruption policies
*   Responsible sourcing

Strong documentation and evidence are essential for achieving higher scores.

### What Is the EcoVadis 360° Watch Monitoring System?

+

EcoVadis 360° Watch is an AI-driven monitoring system that tracks sustainability-related controversies and ESG risks linked to companies.

*   Corruption allegations
*   Labor violations
*   Environmental incidents
*   ESG-related legal disputes

Significant controversies may affect a company’s EcoVadis rating or medal eligibility.
