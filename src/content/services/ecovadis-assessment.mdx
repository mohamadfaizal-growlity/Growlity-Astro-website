### What is EcoVadis and why is it important?

EcoVadis is a globally trusted sustainability ratings platform that evaluates the Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) performance of companies across supply chains. It assesses businesses on four key themes—Environment, Labour & Human Rights, Ethics, and Sustainable Procurement. A strong EcoVadis rating enhances your company’s reputation, strengthens stakeholder trust, supports responsible sourcing, and opens doors to new business opportunities, especially with multinational clients demanding transparency and accountability.

### How does the new EcoVadis scoring and medal system work?

EcoVadis now uses a medal and badge-based recognition system instead of numerical scores. Companies are assessed across four sustainability themes—Environment, Labour & Human Rights, Ethics, and Sustainable Procurement. Based on performance, organizations may earn medals (Bronze, Silver, Gold, or Platinum) or theme-specific badges that highlight excellence in one or more areas. This new approach provides a clearer, more comparable way to benchmark ESG maturity while encouraging continuous improvement.
