## What is ESG Assurance?

ESG Assurance is an independent verification process that confirms the accuracy, credibility, and completeness of your sustainability disclosures, whether it’s your Greenhouse Gas (GHG) inventory, Business Responsibility and Sustainability Report (BRSR), or Annual Sustainability Report (ASR).  
  
Using globally recognized standards like the AA1000 Standard (AA1000AS v3), third-party validates your data and builds stakeholder confidence. It also prepares your organization for regulatory scrutiny, investor evaluation, and global sustainability rankings. At Growlity, we offer independent assurance services tailored to your sustainability reporting needs, delivering credibility, transparency, and audit readiness across your journey.

### What is AA1000 Assurance?

AA1000 Assurance is a globally recognized standard used to independently verify ESG disclosures. It assesses your report’s credibility based on principles of inclusivity, materiality, responsiveness, and impact.

### Why is third-party ESG assurance important?

It increases confidence in your ESG data, improves credibility, supports regulatory compliance, and enhances investor trust.

### What kind of data can be assured?

Commonly assured elements include GHG emissions, sustainability KPIs, BRSR indicators, and narratives in ASRs.

### What’s the difference between limited and reasonable assurance?

Limited assurance offers moderate verification and is less detailed, while reasonable assurance involves deeper testing and offers high confidence in data accuracy.

### Can Growlity provide both advisory and assurance?

Yes. While we maintain third-party integrity in assurance engagements, we also offer separate pre-assurance gap reviews and readiness services.
