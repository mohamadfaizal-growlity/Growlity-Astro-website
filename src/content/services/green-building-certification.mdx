## What is the S&P ESG Score?

The S&P ESG Score by S&P Global measures a company’s environmental, social, and governance performance using detailed, sector-specific, data-driven analysis. It’s widely used by global investors and across major indices like the S&P 500 to assess ESG risks, opportunities, and maturity.

### What is green building certification and why do I need it?

It validates your building’s sustainability and energy performance. It enhances value, reduces environmental impact, and ensures regulatory and ESG compliance.

### What are the types of green building certifications available?

Popular types include LEED, IGBC, BREEAM, EDGE, and GRIHA, each with unique scopes and levels.

### What is the cost of LEED certification or IGBC certification?

Costs vary based on the building type, certification level, and region. We offer a cost-benefit analysis and help you optimize certification ROI.

### Can Growlity help with documentation and credit strategy?

Yes. We manage the complete documentation, from energy simulations to material declarations and policy drafting.

### How long does it take to get certified?

Typically, it takes 6–18 months depending on project size and rating system. We streamline the process to reduce delays.
