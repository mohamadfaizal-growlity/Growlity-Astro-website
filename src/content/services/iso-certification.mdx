### What is ISO 14001 and Why is it Important?

ISO 14001 sets the global benchmark for Environmental Management Systems (EMS). It helps reduce environmental impact, improve regulatory compliance, and boost ESG ratings like EcoVadis or CDP. Ideal for companies seeking environmental leadership and sustainability certification.

### How Can ISO 14064 Help with GHG & CBAM Compliance?

ISO 14064 supports accurate GHG emissions reporting (Scope 1, 2 & 3) and aligns with the GHG Protocol. It strengthens CBAM submissions and enables carbon disclosure for CDP and SBTi. Growlity ensures seamless ISO 14064 integration with ESG software tools.

### What’s the Difference Between ISO 14040 and ISO 14044?

Both support Life Cycle Assessment (LCA): ISO 14040: Sets the LCA framework and principles ISO 14044: Details the methodology and requirements for each LCA phase Essential for credible Product Carbon Footprints (PCF) and Environmental Product Declarations (EPD).

### How Long Does ISO Certification Take?

Typical timeline: Pre-assessment: 2–4 weeks Implementation: 1–3 months Audit & certification: 2–4 weeks Most companies get ISO certified within 3–6 months. Growlity speeds up your journey with tailored guidance.
