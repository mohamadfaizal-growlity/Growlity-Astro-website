## What is ESG Assurance?

ESG Assurance is an independent verification process that confirms the accuracy, credibility, and completeness of your sustainability disclosures, whether it’s your Greenhouse Gas (GHG) inventory, Business Responsibility and Sustainability Report (BRSR), or Annual Sustainability Report (ASR).  
  
Using globally recognized standards like the AA1000 Standard (AA1000AS v3), third-party validates your data and builds stakeholder confidence. It also prepares your organization for regulatory scrutiny, investor evaluation, and global sustainability rankings. ### What is the difference between carbon neutral and Net Zero?

Carbon neutrality refers to balancing emitted and offset emissions, often achieved by purchasing carbon credits to compensate for total emissions. It doesn’t always require deep internal reductions. Net Zero, however, requires significant emissions reductions across your entire value chain first, and only then allows for offsetting unavoidable residual emissions using high-quality, permanent removals. Net Zero is science-based and aligned with global climate targets like the Paris Agreement.
