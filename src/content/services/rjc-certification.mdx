## What is ESG Assurance?

ESG Assurance is an independent verification process that confirms the accuracy, credibility, and completeness of your sustainability disclosures, whether it’s your Greenhouse Gas (GHG) inventory, Business Responsibility and Sustainability Report (BRSR), or Annual Sustainability Report (ASR).  
  
Using globally recognized standards like the AA1000 Standard (AA1000AS v3), third-party validates your data and builds stakeholder confidence. It also prepares your organization for regulatory scrutiny, investor evaluation, and global sustainability rankings. ### What is RJC Certification?

It is a globally accepted certification for ethical, social, environmental, and responsible business practices in the jewellery supply chain.

### Who needs RJC Certification?

Jewellery manufacturers, diamond traders, retailers, and refiners aiming to improve ESG performance or become preferred suppliers.

### How long is the certification valid?

RJC certification is valid for 3 years and includes a mid-term review.

### Is RJC membership mandatory for certification?

Yes, you must first become a member of the Responsible Jewellery Council to apply for certification.

### What is the full form of RJC?

RJC stands for Responsible Jewellery Council. The Responsible Jewellery Council (RJC) is an international, non-profit organization that sets sustainability, ethical, social, and environmental standards for the global jewellery supply chain.
