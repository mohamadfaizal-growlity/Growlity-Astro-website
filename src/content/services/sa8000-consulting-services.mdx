## **What is SA8000?**

SA8000 is one of the most recognized social accountability frameworks focused on workplace ethics, labor rights, employee welfare, and responsible business practices. It provides organizations with a structured approach to managing social performance across operations and supply chains.  
  
The SA8000 framework focuses on areas such as:  
– Child labor prevention  
– Forced labor prevention  
– Workplace health and safety  
– Freedom of association  
– Fair disciplinary practices  
– Working hours management  
– Fair remuneration  
– Management systems for social accountability Child labor prevention

Global procurement teams and international customers increasingly evaluate suppliers based on workplace ethics, employee welfare, and responsible business operations.  
  
Businesses with stronger social accountability systems are often viewed as:  
– More reliable supply chain partners  
– Lower operational risk suppliers  
– Better aligned with ESG expectations  
– More transparent and accountable organizations  
– Long-term strategic sourcing partners
