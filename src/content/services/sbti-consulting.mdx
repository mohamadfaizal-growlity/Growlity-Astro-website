### ESG Reporting

ESG Reporting refers to the structured disclosure of a company’s Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) performance. It enables businesses to demonstrate how they manage risks and opportunities across climate change, resource efficiency, human rights, ethical governance, diversity, and more. In today’s investor-driven, regulation-focused environment, ESG reporting is no longer optional—it’s a strategic imperative. ESG disclosures influence investment decisions, consumer trust, regulatory compliance, and corporate reputation.
