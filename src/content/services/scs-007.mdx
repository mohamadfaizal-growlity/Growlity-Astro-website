### What does SCS-007 certify?

It certifies responsible sourcing, traceability, and climate neutrality for gemstones and jewelry—ensuring environmental and social accountability.

### Is traceability mandatory under SCS-007?

Yes. Full traceability from source to market is a core requirement and includes ownership transfers, declarations, and process transparency.

### Do I need carbon offsets for climate-neutral certification?

Yes. You’ll need a verified carbon footprint and offset strategy. We help calculate emissions and select eligible offset projects.

### What kind of businesses need SCS-007?

Gemstone and jewelry exporters, traders, manufacturers, and even mines seeking global market credibility benefit from this certification.
