21st July, 2025

At **Growlity,** we hosted an exclusive global webinar on **“Ask Me Anything on UNGC CoP 2025.”**

This interactive session provided participants with practical insights on the **United Nations Global Compact Communication on Progress (UNGC CoP) 2025** framework. The discussion focused on understanding the updated reporting requirements, aligning sustainability strategies with the UNGC principles, and preparing organizations to confidently submit their CoP.

**Key Highlights of the Webinar:**

*   Demystifying the **UNGC CoP 2025 reporting framework**
*   Guidance on preparing and submitting the Communication on Progress
*   Insights on aligning business practices with the **Ten Principles of UNGC**
*   Practical advice on overcoming challenges in sustainability disclosure
*   Real-time Q&A addressing participant concerns

This **“Ask Me Anything”** format ensured that organizations gained clarity, avoided common mistakes, and walked away with actionable strategies to strengthen their **ESG reporting and sustainability journey.**
