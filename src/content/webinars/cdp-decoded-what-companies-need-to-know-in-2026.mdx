**The Reality in 2026**

The CDP is no longer just a disclosure platform—it’s a **business performance signal**.

Your CDP score now influences:

*   Investor decisions
*   Customer trust
*   Supply chain eligibility

The gap between **disclosure and strategy** is where most companies fall behind.

If you’re unsure:

This webinar gives you **clarity + direction in under 60 minutes**.
