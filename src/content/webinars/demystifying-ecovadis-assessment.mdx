Join us for an **interactive global webinar on “Demystifying Life Cycle Assessment (LCA) and Environmental Product Declarations (EPDs)”**—a must-attend session for businesses aiming to enhance product transparency, improve sustainability reporting, and gain a competitive advantage in the market.

This exclusive session will **unpack the fundamentals of LCA and EPDs** and provide participants with practical insights to apply in real-world scenarios. Our moderators and experts will guide you through the **key aspects of LCA and EPDs**, explaining why they matter, how they are conducted, and how organizations can leverage them to demonstrate credible sustainability performance.

Whether you are **new to LCA/EPDs or looking to strengthen your existing practices**, this webinar will help you gain actionable knowledge, avoid common pitfalls, and prepare your organization for effective ESG reporting. You’ll also have the opportunity to interact with speakers and learn from their experiences across multiple projects.

By the end of the session, you will walk away with:

*   A clear understanding of **Life Cycle Assessment (LCA) and Environmental Product Declarations (EPDs)**
    
*   Insights into how **LCA data is converted into credible EPDs**
    
*   Practical solutions to challenges faced in real projects
    
*   Understanding how **EPDs support ESG reporting, regulatory compliance, and market differentiation**
    

**Don’t miss this opportunity to learn, engage, and elevate your sustainability journey.**
