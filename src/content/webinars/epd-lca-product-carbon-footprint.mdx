An Expert‑Led Webinar by Growlity | 27 May 2026 | 3:30–4:305 PM IST (Online)

Join **Growlity’s EPD and LCA experts** for a focused, practical session on how to **turn LCA data into market‑ready Product Carbon Footprint (PCF) and Environmental Product Declarations (EPDs)** directly aligned with Scope 3, GHG Protocol, and EU‑style product‑level disclosure requirements.

**What You’ll Learn in 60 Minutes**

*   **EPD frameworks decoded**: How ISO 14025, PCRs, and programme operators shape your EPD strategy and which ones suit your sector.
*   **LCA that drives decisions**: System boundaries, functional units, and hotspot analysis for industrial and chemical products.
*   **From LCA to PCF:** Turn EPD‑aligned LCA into product‑level carbon footprint metrics that feed into Scope 3 and ESG reporting.
*   **Real‑world EPD value**: How manufacturing and specialty‑product companies use EPDs to win tenders, de‑risk green procurement, and strengthen ESG narratives.

**Who Should Attend?**

This session is for:

*   Sustainability & ESG managers evaluating EPDs and PCF for products
*   LCA practitioners and ESG consultants working on Scope 3 and product‑level data
*   Product, procurement, and sales leaders who need to respond to EPD‑linked RFPs and EU‑style green‑procurement requirements

**Why Join Growlity’s Expert Webinar?**

Growlity already helps industrial clients with **EPD development, LCA, and product‑sustainability reporting.** This webinar gives you a **clear, practitioner‑level roadmap** without excessive jargon.
