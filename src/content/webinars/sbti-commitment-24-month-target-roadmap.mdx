Join this expert‑led webinar on 13 May 2026 (3:30–4:30 PM IST) to learn how to manage your SBTi commitment effectively, stay inside the 24‑month window, and build a clear, actionable path from commitment to validated science‑based targets.

**What You’ll Learn**

*   Understand the SBTi commitment window: How the 24‑month countdown starts and what happens if targets are not submitted on time.
*   Map your roadmap: From commitment letter to baseline, target‑setting, and validation—what to prioritise and when.
*   Avoid common pitfalls: Inadequate baselines, inconsistent Scope 1–3 coverage, and weak documentation that delay or block SBTi‑aligned targets.
*   Align SBTi with your ESG strategy: How to turn SBTi commitments into internal projects, budgets, and buyer‑facing climate narratives.
    

**Who Should Attend?**

*   ESG, sustainability, and decarbonisation leads
    
*   Finance, energy, and manufacturing teams supporting SBTi‑linked reductions
    
*   Procurement and CSR professionals responding to global‑buyer climate‑requirement queries
