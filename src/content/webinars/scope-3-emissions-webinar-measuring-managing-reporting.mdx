### Your biggest carbon footprint may not be inside your organization.  

For many companies, more than 70% of total greenhouse gas emissions come from their value chain — suppliers, transportation, purchased goods, product use, and end-of-life activities. Yet Scope 3 remains one of the most complex and misunderstood areas of carbon accounting.

As regulations tighten, customer expectations grow, and sustainability reporting becomes a business imperative, organizations can no longer afford to overlook Scope 3 emissions.

Join this practical and insightful webinar to understand how leading organizations are identifying, measuring, managing, and reporting Scope 3 emissions with confidence.

### What You’ll Uncover:  

✔ Understanding the 15 Scope 3 emission categories

✔ Common challenges in Scope 3 data collection and calculation

✔ Practical approaches to supplier engagement and data management

✔ Key reporting requirements and global frameworks

✔ Strategies to reduce value chain emissions and drive business value

✔ Real-world examples and industry best practices

### Who Should Attend?  

*   Sustainability & ESG Professionals

*   Supply Chain & Procurement Teams
    
*   Corporate Reporting Professionals
