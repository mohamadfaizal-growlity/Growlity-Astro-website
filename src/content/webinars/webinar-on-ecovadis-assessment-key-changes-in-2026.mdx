**What’s New? What’s Reweighted. What Assessors Expect.**

**A focused walkthrough of EcoVadis 2026 updates and how to respond.**

EcoVadis 2026 strengthens expectations around **evidence quality, supply-chain due diligence, and regulatory alignment**. This session covers only what has changed or gained importance  so you can update your approach without overworking your submission.

**Why This Matters in 2026 ?**

EcoVadis now evaluates

*   **Impact over intent**
*   **Active due diligence over basic screening** 
*   **Integrated governance over siloed documents**  

Old strategies = stagnant scores.

**Key 2026 EcoVadis Shift**

*   **Stronger Evidence Credibility** : KPI, Targets, Trends, Site-level data
*   **Deeper Supplier Due Diligence** : Risk Prioritization, Engagement, Corrective Action
*   **Cross-theme Integration:** Environment, L&HR, Ethics, Procurement

**What You’ll Learn**

*   What assessors prioritise in 2026
*   Why scores stall at **50–55**
*   Challenges throughout the assessment
*   What to update in existing submissions

**Who Should Attend**

*   ESG & Sustainability teams
*   Procurement & Supply Chain leaders
*   Compliance, Risk & Ethics teams
*   Operations, EHS & Quality heads
*   Leaders accountable for EcoVadis ratings
